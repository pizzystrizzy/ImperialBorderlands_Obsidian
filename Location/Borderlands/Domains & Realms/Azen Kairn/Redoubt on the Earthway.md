**Redoubt on the Earthway**. The Cesta Ithan, or Earthway, is a subterranean river that travels from its source near [[Azen Kairn|Azen Kairn]] southeast to eventually connect with the [[Mirmen River|Mirmen]] southwest of [[Türos Tellë]]. When dwarves dwelled in Azen Kairn, the Earthway was an underground thoroughfare for shipping to and from the great vault and provided the hydraulic power for its intricate machineries. With beastmen marauders an ever-present threat, the dwarves constructed a number of redoubts to protect the shipping lane. This redoubt was one of the largest, a formidable bastion that guarded the confluence of the Earthway and the Mirmen. When Azen Kairn fell to the fires of the red dragon [[Orm]] during the Beastman Wars, the redoubt on the Earthway was lost to Orm’s forces. Now it is ruled by Orm’s daughter **Nindukugga**, and a **tribe of ogres** that serves her.
