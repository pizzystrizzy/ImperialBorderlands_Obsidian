Another of the border forts built by Ulkyreus, **Türos Drav ("Defense-Castle")** guards a major ford on the [[Krysivor River]] that leads into the [[Madoan Hills]] and the wilderness beyond. On clear days, the cliffs of the Dark Wall are visible from the fort’s watchtowers. The fort’s commander, Legate Jonus Tacillus, is obsessed with war, martial drill, and military history – much to the chagrin of all the soldiers posted to Drav. He is immensely frustrated at being posted here, instead of on the front lines of the war against the Skysos and will practically welcome bad news of invasion from the Waste. Türos Drav makes an excellent home base from which adventurers can explore the Madoan Hills and [[The Dark Wall]].
