**Zelictum ("Fort Vengeance")** is a 300-year old fort constructed after the Second Krysean Rebellion around the same time as [[Andrastem]] and [[Sarotem]]. Tarkaun Audarius XI Ulkyreus repurposed it as a third-line fort for the Borderlands and stationed a cavalry reserve here. The fort’s garrison has largely been sent west, but the fort’s elderly commander, Legate Manthus Mirasca, remained behind. Mirasca is lame in both legs and deaf in one ear, but his mind is still sharp and his tactical acumen among the best in the Borderlands.
