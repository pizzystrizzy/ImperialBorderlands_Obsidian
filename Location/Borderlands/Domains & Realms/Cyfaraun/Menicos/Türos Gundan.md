**Türos Gundan ("Castle of Wolves")** is part of the line of forts built 150 years ago by Tarkaun Audarius XI Ulkyreus to provide a forward defense against beastman invaders from beyond [[The Dark Wall]]. The fort perches on a large hill from where it commands both the road junction to the northwest and a narrow ford across the [[Krysivor River|Krysivor]] to the southeast. Lately, well-organized warbands of orcs (from the [[Lair of the Warlock]]) have been ferrying across the river to burn Türos Gundan’s watchtowers and raid its supply caravans. Legate Marcan Hilerno would eagerly accept the assistance of adventurers willing to cross the river and deal with the orcs.
