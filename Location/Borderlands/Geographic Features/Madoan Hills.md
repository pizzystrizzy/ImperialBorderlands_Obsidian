This range of limestone foothills runs northeast from the [[Meniri Mountains|Meniri]] towards the forests. A dense heath of sage, juniper, and myrtle densely covers the arid, rocky hills. In spring, wild rockrose and thorny broom turn the hillsides white, pink, and yellow. The **Madoan Hills ("Yellow"**) were frequently acclaimed in the hymns of Argollë and Zahar for their beauty, but the looming dangers of [[The Dark Wall]] leave them nowadays unsettled and little traveled.
