The headwaters of the **Blood River** rise from mountain springs high in the [[Meniri Mountains]]. The nearby slopes were long ago denuded of their forests by the dwarves of Azen Khador, and in the rainy months the Blood River is stained a deep redorange by eroded sediment. The Blood River is about 100 yards wide for most of its length and has an average depth of 10 feet. The confluence of the Blood River and [[Krysivor River|Krysivor]], known as the Bleeding Point, was the site of Aedon Lann’s most famous victory in the Beastman Wars. Sediment accumulation has left the confluence fairly shallow, and the Bleeding Point becomes fordable during droughts.
