The **Viaspen Forest ("Lily Forest")** is a primeval forest of cedar, yew, and oak trees situated between the [[Viamir Marshes]] and [[Krysivor River]]. Near the Krysivor elm and poplar trees grow, while the trees to the west give way to willows, sages, and rushes as the forest transitions into wetland. The Viaspen was safe when the elves ruled the land but is wild and dangerous now.
