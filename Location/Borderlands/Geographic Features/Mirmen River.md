Taking its name from its dark waters and black soil, the **Mirmen River ("Black Rock")** begins in the peaks of the [[Meniri Mountains|Meniri]] to the south and travels some 300 miles before emptying into the Ammas Aurë. The river, which ranges from 100 yards to 1 mile wide, runs quite deep (50 to 150 feet) and has a slow, steady current. The banks are marshy, with narrow-tailed cattails and reed thickets.
