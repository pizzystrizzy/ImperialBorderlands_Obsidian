A range of fierce rugged volcanic peaks, the **Meniri Mountains ("Mountains of Rock")** run east from Southern Argollë to Kemesh, over 1,000 miles. They separate, and protect, the fertile lands of the Ammas Aurë from the Great Waste to the south. The lower slopes of the Meniri are thickly forested with tall cedar trees, while ash, evergreen oak, and pine dominate on the thin rocky soils of the upper slopes. The high peaks are snowcapped year round.
