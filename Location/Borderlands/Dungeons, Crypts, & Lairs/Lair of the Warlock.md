**Lair of the Warlock**. The ruins of a centuries-old Argollëan keep in the [[Istrith Forest]] has been occupied by the sinister warlock **Narsürius** and his apprentices and servants. The warlock’s orc minions frequently raid supply caravans along the [[Krysivor River]], and have been probing the defenses of [[Türos Gundan]], but Narsürius’s true objectives lie to the south: [[Ruins of Zahar|Zahar]], where an ancient power stirs.
