This is a limestone cavern on a forested hill. Dank and dark, the bones of dozens of beasts and men litter the main cave. At the close of the Argollëan War, elves from the [[Istrith Forest]] used this cavern as a storehouse for treasures evacuated from the [[Ruined City of Cyfandir]], intending to return later to reclaim it. They never did, and the treasure remains – 500gp and 2 ceremonial swords with goldleaf blades (50gp value each) inside a locked steel chest. The chest is engraved with leaf motif and the coins are stamped with the image of the Argollëan High Queen and dated to the time of the Argollëan War. The cave is now the lair of a vicious **pride of 4 manticores** that has taken to preying on sailors and fishermen on the coast. It is their depredations that have earned the ire of Legate Natherian at [[Türos Aurë]].
