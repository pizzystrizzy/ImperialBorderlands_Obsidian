A massive natural monolith, about 1200 feet long and 600 feet wide, rises 700 feet high out of the flat and barren ground in [[The Waste]]. The rock is smooth from years of erosion, save for the three great cracks that run along its southeastern face. These cracks cause the monolith to resemble a closed fist, and give the rock formation its name: **The Fist of Telith**. The remains of an ancient temple of Telith are visible atop the Fist, and accessible via a staircase cut into the rock of the southwest face. Adventurers who ascend to the top of the Fist find the temple has collapsed into rubble. Those who take the time to excavate the rubble (5gp construction project) gain access to the dungeon below the temple, where treasures of the old cult still lie. The Fist of Telith is a very large *enduring edifice of elemental earth* whose powers are available to anyone atop or inside the monolith (720,000 square feet).
