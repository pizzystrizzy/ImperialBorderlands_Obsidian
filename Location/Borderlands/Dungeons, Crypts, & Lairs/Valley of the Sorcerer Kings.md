**Valley of the Sorcerer Kings**. The Zaharan sorcerer-king **Memnhisir** chose to await the Awakening in a majestic tomb excavated in this hidden valley. His successor followed, and thereafter the valley became the burial ground for all of Zahar’s rulers, high priests, and viziers. Over the next two centuries six successive sorcerer-kings were interred in the valley: **Djarghul**, **Negirau**, **Ankhare**, **Balathos**, **Sahura**, and **Mentu**. And when Zahar fell to the bronze-armored legions of Aura the last sorcerer-king, **Sebek**, the came here to transition to unlife before taking command of the [[Ruins of Zahar|Zahar]]. The Valley of the Sorcerer-Kings is exceptionally isolated and difficult to find; adventurers have a -6 penalty to discover it when searching unless they have a map.
