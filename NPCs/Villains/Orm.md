Orm is a red dragon who conquered [[Azen Kairn]] in 225 BE, where he rules along with his brood of lesser dragons and his army of beastmen. His hoard is said to be the largest in Aurëpos.

His vassal Turtak is an ogre warlord who commands a warband at the [[Watchtower at Eammon Mór]]. 

His daughter Nindukugga is another vassal who commands a tribe of ogres at the [[Redoubt on the Earthway]].
