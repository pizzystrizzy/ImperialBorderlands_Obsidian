## Wilderness Expeditions

### ACKS Sequence of Play

1. Determine weather
![[weather_table.png|537x358]]
If weather conditions are Frigid, Foggy, Muddy, Snowy, or Sweltering, expedition speed will be halved. If weather conditions are Flurry, Foggy, Rainy, Snowy, Sunbaked, Stormy, or Windy, penalties might apply to certain activities. Effects of multiple conditions are cumulative (RR 245).

2. Check for Starvation and Dehydration

3. Determine activities & marching order

4. Check for Daily Encounters. If the party will be hunting or resting in unsettled territory, roll to see if any random encounters will occur that day. If a random encounter will occur, roll 1d12 to determine the hour of its occurrence

5. Check to see if the party gets lost (RR 275). If they get lost, determine the direction they will erroneously be traveling. 

6. Adjudicate each hour. For each of the 12 hours:
	a. Check for Scheduled Encounter
	b. Adjudicate Special Activities (anything besides search, traveling, survival, resting)
	c. Adjudicate Travel and Encounters.  If the party is traveling, adjust its position on your wilderness map based on one hour of travel in the appropriate direction. If this causes the party to enter a new hex, roll to see if a random encounter occurs. If an encounter occurs, follow the encounter procedure (below).
	d. Adjudicate Searching. If any adventurers in the party are searching a hex, roll to see if a random encounter occurs. If an encounter occurs, follow the encounter procedure (below). After the encounter (if any), is resolved, roll to see if their search finds any lairs.
	e. Adjudicate Weather Effects. (specifically frigid and sweltering)

7. Resolve Foraging and Hunting

8. Consume Rations. Mark off no, half, or full food ration and no, half, or full water ration for each creature on the expedition.
	a. If underfed eats full ration, condition ends.
	b. if starving eats full ration, becomes underfed.
	c. If dehydrated drinks full ration of water, condition ends.
	d. 2 days without food, or 5 days with half rations, = underfed
	e. If underfed for 5 days, or 10 days with half rations, =- starving. Day without water or 2 days with half-water = dehydrated.

9. Adjudicate Weather effects
	a. Disease (JJ 84)
	b. Hypothermia
	c. Mud -- if drizzly for 3 days or rainy today, grasslands & scrublands = muddy. Cold, frigid, or sweltering, or moderate x 7 days = mud dries
	d. Snow -- if flurry for 3 days or snowy today, terrain = snowy. If sweltering today, or moderate x 7 days = snow melts.
	e. Sunburn -- if sunbaked, no protective clothing -> fire damage

10. Adjudicate Fatigue

11. Adjudicate Healing

12. Check for Nighttime Encounters.

13. Advance calendar by 1 day.
